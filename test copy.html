Why people struggle
  • Get enough sleep before test.
  • Have a good breakfast in the morning.
  • Study properly, take notes on the sections you are going to be tested on.
  • Read the instructions carefully.
  • Focus on 1 question at a time.
  • Relax, don't be so nervous that you will blank out.
  • Don't stay stuck on the question too long, time will be wasted.
  • Do the easier questions first.
  • Don't rush, pace yourself.
  • Don't get stuck on a question too long, skip it and do it later if you don't know the answer.
